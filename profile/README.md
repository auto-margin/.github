### + About Auto Margin
Auto Margin is a technology-driven automotive analytics company building an advanced pricing intelligence platform for professional car dealers across Europe.

We combine real-time market data, predictive pricing models and automated margin calculations to help dealerships make faster, safer and more profitable purchasing and sales decisions.

---

###  + What We’re Building
- 🚗 A next-generation automotive price analytics platform  
- 📊 Real-time market benchmarking across European marketplaces  
- 💰 Automated margin and profitability calculations  
- 📈 Advanced pricing insights powered by AI and structured data  
- 🔐 Secure dealer-focused analytics ecosystem  

---

### + Tech Stack

We build with performance, scalability and data accuracy in mind.

**Core:** `TypeScript` `Node.js` `React` `pnpm`<br/>
**Database & Storage:** `PostgreSQL` `Prisma` `Supabase` `AWS (S3 RDS EFS CloudFront)`<br/>
**Backend & Infrastructure:** `Express` `AWS Lambda` `CI/CD Pipelines`<br/>
**Frontend:** `React 19` `Vite` `Tailwind CSS`<br/>
**Data & Intelligence:** `AI-driven Pricing Models` `Market Scraping` `Analytics Engine` `Playwright` `Puppeteer`<br/>
**DevOps:** `GitHub Actions` `Docker` `Cloud CDN`<br/>
